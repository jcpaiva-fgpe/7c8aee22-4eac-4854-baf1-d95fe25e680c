Combine Emission Sources

Industry and transport are major contributors to rising emissions. The GCIA wants to see their combined impact across several years.

  1. You are given two arrays, for example:
    source1 = [120, 135, 150]
    source2 = [80, 90, 95]
  2. Convert both arrays into NumPy arrays.
  3. Add them element-wise to calculate the total emissions for each year.
  4. Return the resulting NumPy array to report the totals.
