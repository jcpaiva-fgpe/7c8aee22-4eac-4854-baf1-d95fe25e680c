City Registry

The agency is expanding its watchlist of cities. You are asked to build a simple registry that stores both names and their warming values.

  1. Create a list with the cities: ["Berlin", "Madrid", "Sydney"].
  2. Create a dictionary with warming values: {"Berlin": 0.9, "Madrid": 1.2, "Sydney": 0.8}.
  3. Then, print the list and the dictionary so the registry is visible.
